<p><p>there is a monster in me, too. i feel it;<br />
a meanness, growing like a tree<br />
in my belly.</p><p>is it hidden-in my long fingered hands<br />
in my hair the color of blood?</p><p>in my wildfire love<br />

i am as bloodthirsty as my father<br />
and that was not thread you unwound</p><p>i&rsquo;ll have you know-<br />
