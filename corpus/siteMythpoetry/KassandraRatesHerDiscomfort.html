kassandra rates her discomfort on a scale from 1-10

alex peery clark

Q: you say you can see the future?

A: i can't see the future, i just know it. i don't know it, it's a feeling. it's not a feeling, it's an urge. it's like a headache. it's like heartburn. i't's like a stomach flu. you wouldn't wish it on anyone but you wish they knew how it felt.

Q: how long have you been experiencing theses symptoms?

A: i know i wasn't always like this but i can't remember anything else.
