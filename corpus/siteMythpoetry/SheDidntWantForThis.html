(!!!) she didn’t want for this

i.m.a.

ANONYME ASKED:

(!!!) she didn’t want for this / but they dragged her away / kicking and screaming and resisting / place a crown of roses devoid of petals / upon her pretty head / said pain makes a leader / but now / they are the ones / kicking and screaming and resisting / when she presses the thorns / that were once resting / in her pretty golden locks / into the red of their wounds / which are the same red / of the petals the once discarded / in favor of thorns / to adorn her head

(i.m.a.)
